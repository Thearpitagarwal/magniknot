## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is anti-tarnish jewellery?**
Anti-tarnish jewellery is coated with a protective layer that prevents the metal from
oxidising (turning dark or discoloured). MagniKnot's pieces use 18K gold plating plus
an anti-tarnish topcoat.

**Q: How long does it last?**
1–2 years with regular daily wear and basic care (avoid soaking, perfume contact, and
harsh chemicals).

**Q: Is it safe for sensitive skin?**
Yes. The 18K gold plating and anti-tarnish coating act as a barrier, making it suitable
for most people with metal sensitivities.
